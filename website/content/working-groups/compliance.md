## About

The Compliance working group addresses the intersection of cloud native technology and regulatory compliance. As organizations adopt cloud native architectures, they must comply with privacy and data protection laws while ensuring compute and data integrity. This group bridges technical implementations with the legal and regulatory workflows needed for audits and certifications.

## Goals

- Curate vendor-neutral guidance on compliance in cloud native environments
- Address auditability, non-repudiation, and forensic evidence collection
- Map technical controls to common regulatory frameworks (NIST, PCI-DSS, SOC 2, etc.)
- Provide patterns for generating reviewable artifacts from cloud native systems

## Focus Areas

- Privacy and data protection compliance
- Audit readiness for cloud native deployments
- Regulatory mapping to cloud native controls
- Automated evidence generation for compliance
