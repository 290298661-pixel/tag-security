## About

The Software Supply Chain Security working group tackles one of the most critical challenges in cloud native security: ensuring the integrity and security of the software supply chain. With the ever-growing importance of open source software and the increasing sophistication of supply chain attacks, this group produces best practices, whitepapers, and reference architectures.

## Goals

- Define and promote best practices for software supply chain security
- Provide reference architectures for secure software delivery
- Develop assessment frameworks for evaluating supply chain risk
- Maintain the [Catalog of Supply Chain Compromises](https://github.com/cncf/tag-security/tree/main/community/catalog/compromises)
