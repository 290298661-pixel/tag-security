## About

The Research working group provides a structured process for TAG Security members to publish articles, blog posts, and thought leadership pieces. It helps contributors turn ideas and expertise into polished publications that reach the broader cloud native community.

The group aims to publish 3–4 articles per year, covering topics such as emerging threats, security best practices, project deep-dives, and lessons learned from real-world incidents.

## Example Publications

- [Security of Software Update Systems in 2023](https://thenewstack.io/security-of-software-update-systems-in-2023/) (The New Stack)

## How to Propose an Article

1. Join the `#tag-security` channel on [CNCF Slack](https://slack.cncf.io/)
2. Share your article idea with the group
3. Write your draft in markdown
4. Submit it as a PR to the [website/content/blog](https://github.com/cncf/tag-security/tree/main/website/content/blog) directory
5. The group will review, provide feedback, and schedule publication

See the [blog guidelines](https://github.com/cncf/tag-security/blob/main/governance/blog-guidelines.md) for detailed instructions.
